About Our Institution

Our Mission

A Catholic educational institution run by the Presentation Sisters of the Blessed Virgin Mary in the Philippines. Inspired by the mission of Christ and the charism of Venerable Nano Nagle that upholds Catholic ideals and provides quality, holistic and transformative education to 21st century learners.

Our Vision

Christ-centered advocates of excellence, social justice, stewardship and societal transformation in a rapidly changing world.

Core Values

Faith, Service, Integrity, and Excellence in everything we do.

Academic Programs

Information Technology

Develop skills in software development, networking, cybersecurity, and database management to thrive in the digital world.

Business Administration

Learn the fundamentals of management, marketing, finance, and entrepreneurship to lead organizations effectively.

Education

Shape the next generation through a program focused on teaching strategies, child development, and values-based learning.

Hospitality Management

Gain expertise in hotel operations, food service, tourism, and event management for a career in the global hospitality industry.

Criminology

Study criminal justice, law enforcement, forensic science, and public safety to serve and protect the community.

Explore Our Departments

Binalbagan Catholic College offers a wide range of academic departments designed to nurture every learner — from elementary to college level. Discover the department that fits your goals and passion.

  • World-class faculty
  • Faith-based curriculum
  • Modern facilities
  • Holistic student development

College Department

Our College Department offers bachelor's degree programs designed to equip students with professional skills and academic excellence.

  • Bachelor of Science in Information Technology
  • Bachelor of Science in Business Administration
  • Bachelor of Science in Criminology
  • Bachelor of Science in Hospitality Management
  • Bachelor of Elementary Education

Senior High School

Senior High School prepares students for college and career through specialized tracks aligned with their interests and goals.

  • Academic Track (STEM, ABM, HUMSS)
  • Technical-Vocational-Livelihood Track
  • Sports Track
  • Arts and Design Track

Junior High School

Junior High School (Grades 7–10) builds a strong academic foundation with values formation and holistic development.

  • Core Subjects (Math, Science, English, Filipino)
  • Technology and Livelihood Education
  • MAPEH (Music, Arts, PE, Health)
  • Values Education & Christian Living

Elementary Department

The Elementary Department (Grades 1–6) nurtures young learners through a child-centered, faith-based curriculum.

  • Language Arts & Literacy
  • Mathematics & Science
  • Araling Panlipunan & Filipino
  • Christian Living & Values Formation
